Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This dashboard allows you to quickly search the DataBase of participants in this study and returns three different visualizations! The bar chart displays the top 10 bacterias found within the participant's belly button. The chart next to the bar chart is a guage that looks at the amount of times a week a participant cleans their belly button. The bubble chart on the bottom compares all of the different bacterias found in the participant's belly button based on frequency of occurance.
